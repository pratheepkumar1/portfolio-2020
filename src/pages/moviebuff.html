Overview

At Moviebuff, you will be able to watch the latest movie trailers, photos, access cast and crew information, cinemas theatres, thus providing a sheer volume of movie-based information. Users’ critical activities include following the movie, cast & crew and the theatres, book ticket, rating and review the movie.

Duration Aug 2017 - Dec 2018
Domain Cinema & Entertainment
Done as Employment Project
My Role “It includes designing the user experience, visual design, interactive prototype and ensure a smooth dev hand off. ”
Qube Cinema Development Arena Wallart, Chennai

FEB 2018
Identifying
Essential vs Necessary

Since there was a demand by the stakeholders to hit the market with the first mobile app of the web product Moviebuff in a span of 6 months, we articulated the immediate and later needs of the products. We also considered the constraints of adopting new technology such as React Native (which was nascent at that time) for the first time in our product suite. So we split into two phases. The first shipment would include movie-based information where the user could only view the content. In the second phase, we decided to include user interactions and personalization.

Essential
Relevance

How might we present relevant movies initially from 10,000+ movies with minimal user effort.

discoverable

How might be ensure every movie is easily discoverable and grouped in a more contextual way.

Informative

How might we prioritize each piece of information about movie, cast & crew and present it to the user without cognitive overloading.

Necessary
Preference

How might we provide personalization to the users and ensure that their preference is emphasized.

Efficiency

How might be provide customization and quickness during the discoverability phase.

Engagement

How might be incorporate user actions from the existing website to the mobile app and improvize the user engagement experience

FEB 2018
Identifying
The Essentials
Location Selection

To provide relevant information, we allowed the user to detect or choose their location to filter out the movies release patterns and showtime availability in their location

Movie Release Timeline and Search

To provide better discoverability, we arranged the information based on release date in a timeline fashion along with language filter and search options

Movie Information

Based on the information architecture, we prioritized the information of the movie page. We rearranged the information based on the life cycle of the movie which is from the announcement of the movie to the archival of it.

What’s New Section

This section in the movie page provides information that are latest and automatically gets updated based on the information getting added to the page.
